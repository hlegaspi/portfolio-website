Introduction

I am a new UI/UX designer that loves coming up with new ideas and is always learning. I studied nursing in college but have found that my interests lie in problem-solving and visual design, which led me to the world of UI/UX. Coming from a healthcare background, I make sure to design with empathy, use my problem-solving skills, and be a team player. One thing I love to do is travel. When I travel, I always like planning meticulously. I like to visit places that are hidden or not as popular to tourists, creating a unique experience. These same skills are what I apply when designing.
